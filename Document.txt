This handbook defines core policies for onboarding, benefits, time-off, and refunds related to training programs.
Refer to each section for authoritative guidance.
When in doubt, contact People Operations.

1. Onboarding Policy
1.1 New Hire Overview

All new hires must complete the onboarding checklist within 10 business days.
The checklist includes:

Account setup

Security training

Equipment verification

1.2 Security Training

Mandatory security training must be completed within the first 7 days of employment.
Failure to complete training may result in temporary access restrictions.

1.3 Equipment

Employees receive a standard company-issued laptop and accessories.
Any exceptions must be approved by the employee’s manager.

2. Refund Policy (Authoritative)
2.1 Scope

This refund policy applies only to company-sponsored external training courses purchased through the Learning & Development (L&D) portal.

2.2 Eligibility

A refund is approved when any of the following conditions are met:

The course provider cancels or reschedules the course beyond 30 days

The employee withdraws at least 14 days before the course start date

The course content is materially different from the published syllabus

2.3 Non-Eligibility

Refunds are not provided in the following cases:

No-shows or withdrawals fewer than 14 days before course start

Personal preference after the course has started

Internal training materials or internal workshops

2.4 Refund Process

Employees must submit a refund request in the L&D portal within 10 business days of the qualifying event.
Required documentation includes:

Proof of purchase

Communication from the course provider

Approved refunds are processed within 15 business days.

3. Time Off and Holidays
3.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)

Employees accrue PTO on a monthly basis.
PTO requests should be submitted at least 7 days in advance.

3.2 Local Holidays

Local holidays follow the regional calendar published by People Operations.

3.3 Sick Leave

Sick leave is available immediately upon employment.
A doctor’s note may be required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

4. Not Covered Topics
4.1 Payroll Banking Changes

Instructions for changing payroll banking details are maintained in a separate Payroll SOP and are not covered in this handbook.

4.2 Hardware Warranty Terms

Hardware warranty policies are documented by the IT department and are not included in this document.
